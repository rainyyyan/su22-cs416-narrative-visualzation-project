JO1 Pre-Debut

After Produce 101 Japan concluded on December 11, 2019, the official twitter account for the final group was revealed to be @official_jo1.

Following the end of the show, members of the newly-formed group JO1 traveled to South Korea to prepare for their official debut. As a result, the official twitter account had brief periods of inactivity during this time.

On January 29th, 2020, the official Twitter account announced that the group's debut single, titled "Protostar", would be released on March 4, 2020. The twitter account became more active as it began to release teasers for the group's debut.
