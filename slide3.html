JO1 After Debut

The plot on the right presents all tweets by the official twitter account after the group's debut, March 4, 2020, up until when the account's twitter data was retrieved on July 10, 2022.

The plot also shows all eps and albums released by the group and the period during which the release was promoted. One can see that the group had a new release every 3-5 months.

Of all present releases, only The Star and Kizuna are studio albums, with the rest of the releases being EPs that contained 5-7 songs each.
